Treatment of bedwetting - for children and adolescents

 A series of treatments that includes diagnosis, training, determination of treatment methods and lending of an electronic device to children and adolescents aged 5-18 years who suffer from bedwetting.
 

Last updated: 01.1.24
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver
Who is eligible?
Children and adolescents aged 5-18 with nocturnal enuresis, referred by a family doctor or paediatrician.
Seniority required in the insurance plan
The service is given to Maccabi Shelli and Maccabi Gold members after 12 months from the date of joining the program.

What is the entitlement?
One series of treatments for life, for children and adolescents aged 5 to 18 years.
 
THE SERIES INCLUDES APPOINTMENTS WITH A THERAPIST FOR DIAGNOSIS, ADJUSTMENT OF TREATMENT METHOD AND BORROWING OF AN ELECTRONIC DEVICE.

What should you do?
For a refund, you must apply for a refund on the Maccabi Online website or in the app and attach the required documents.
If necessary, the documents can also be submitted at an appointment at the medical center or sent by registered mail to the medical center.
 
Required documents:
Referral from a family doctor or children.
Original tax receipt and invoice.
Locating a Medical Center
To explain how to apply for a refund on the website or app

What else is worth knowing?
For some therapists, you have to deposit a deposit on the electronic device they rent. The refund from Maccabi does not include the deposit. At the end of the treatment, you will receive the deposit back from the caregiver.
It is possible to purchase a bell for the treatment of enuresis in the branches of Maccabi Pharm, without a refund.
All of the above is subject to the additional health care regulations in Maccabi.
