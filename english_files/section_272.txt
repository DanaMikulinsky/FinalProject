 Ligaments or splints for punctuation (abduction) for cases of disruption of the development of the hip joint.
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic Basket
Who is eligible?
A member suffering from developmental discharge in the hip joints DDH.

What is the entitlement?
One brace once in a lifetime.
