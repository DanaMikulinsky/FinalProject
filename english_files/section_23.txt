Custom Ear Plugs

 After button implantation surgery or surgery to repair a rupture of the eardrum, earplugs will be fitted to prevent fluid from entering the middle ear. This accessory is tailor-made uniquely for you.
 

Last updated: 01.1.24
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver
Who is eligible?
Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold and Maccabi Silver friends who have had or are about to undergo button surgery or have a ruptured eardrum.

What should you do? Before purchasing, you must obtain from an ENT doctor a referral, which includes a medical diagnosis and a recommendation for the purchase of the accessory. The referral is valid for 30 days.
Reach the supplier with a Maccabi card and ID card to locate a medical center

What else is worth knowing?
The earplugs are individually tailored for each member and made uniquely in the workshop
Eligibility after button surgery will be exercised only as long as the buttons or tubes are in the ear.
Locating a Medical Center
If you are entitled to medical treatment or reimbursement or participation for medical expenses under the following laws, the service will not be provided under the additional health care services:
 
HURT IN HOSTILITIES RECOGNIZED BY SOCIAL SECURITY
Disabled Ministry of Defense 20% or more
Injured in road accidents
 
If the need for the service is not due to the violation recognized by the laws listed above, a letter from a doctor confirming this must be submitted to the office of the medical center (branch), in order to obtain permission for the realization of eligibility.
The letter can be submitted in an envelope to a queue or sent by registered mail or email to the medical center. Locating a Medical Center >
All of the above is subject to the additional health care regulations in Maccabi.
