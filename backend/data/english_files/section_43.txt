Hearing tests for friends who have previously purchased a hearing aid 

 Members who have previously purchased a hearing aid through Maccabi (“innovators”) can be tested at hearing centers under agreement with Maccabi.
 

Last updated: 7/18/23
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic Basket
What is the entitlement?
HEARING TEST AND HEARING REHABILITATION COUNSELING. 

Related entitlements 
Hearing aids from the age of 18 and older

Who is eligible?
Members over the age of 18 who have previously purchased a hearing aid through Maccabi. 

How much does it cost?
No cost. The examination and consultation are included in the self-paid participation on the hearing aid.
