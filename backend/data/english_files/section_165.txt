Breastfeeding Consultant and Telephone Breastfeeding Counseling Center

 Qualified nurses who have undergone appropriate training and have received a license from the Ministry of Health and Health give personal counseling and training at home, in the clinic or in the call center.
 

Last updated: 01.1.24
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic Basket
Who is eligible?
Maccabi friendship after childbirth.
Seniority required in the insurance plan
The service is given to Maccabi Shelli members after 6 months from the date of joining the program.

What is the entitlement?
Maccabi Shelli
2 Meetings with a lactation consultant - in your home or virtually (online)
Duration of the session - one to one and a half hours
Eligibility for the benefit is renewed after each birth, as long as you are a member of Maccabi Shelli
Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic BasketTelephone breastfeeding advice provided by certified nurses with an academic degree, lactation consultants licensed by the Ministry of Health, which includes:
Completion of basic knowledge for the mother about breastfeeding.
Providing help with difficulties and questions about breastfeeding.
Assisting the mother in the proper management of breastfeeding in order to increase the chances of success in the subject and give a good experience to the mother and baby.
One session of breastfeeding counseling at the clinic (the lactation consultant will schedule additional sessions if you feel it is necessary).
How much does it cost?
In the call center
No cost.
In the Nurses Clinic and Milk Dropper
No cost.
In your home with a private lactation consultant (Maccabi Shelli)
First visit - 166₪ deductible
Second visit - 89₪ deductible
Virtual Consultation - 110₪ for the first visit and 89₪ for the second visit

Where is the service provided? 
On the phone
Telephone breastfeeding advice is provided at the Maccabi hotline non-stop *3555. In the call routing system, press 4 to Nurse Hotline and 9 once more to Breastfeeding Hotline.
 
Hours of operation of the hotline
Thu - 8am-3pm
Fri - 8am-1pm
In a nurse's clinic or by a drop of milk
Breastfeeding advice in the clinic is obtained at milk drop stations or in the nurses' clinics on the list

What should you do?
Breastfeeding consultant by phone as part of the Maccabi Basket
CALL THE BREASTFEEDING HOTLINE DURING THE CALL CENTER'S OPERATING HOURS
Outside the office hours - for urgent questions in the field of breastfeeding, please consult a nurse at the 3555 call center*
Private lactation consultant for Maccabi Shelli companiesMake an appointment with a lactation consultant who has an agreement
Present her with a Maccabi card and ID card for identification and eligibility verification
Set up a second consultation 
Pay the deductible directly to the consultant
What else is worth knowing?
Telephone consultation
 
There is no limit on the number of telephone consultations
No prior appointment or referral required
Breastfeeding advice at homeThere is no possibility to replace a counselor between the 2 sessions
If you find it difficult to find a lactation consultant available near your place of residence, you can get a 70% refund of the cost, up to these ceilings:
First Visit - 239.79₪
Second visit - $67.65 To receive confirmation and reimbursement from Maccabi, you must submit to the Maccabi a receipt or original invoice with the license number of the Ministry of Health of the Breastfeeding Consultant, in which the number of visits and the dates of the visits are indicated.
Refunds will be given only subject to approval from Maccabi
 
For full information on refund request
